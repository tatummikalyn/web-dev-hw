Chasing Rainbows

By: Tatum Curry

11/14/14

The day was cold, it was rainy and gloomy.
But when the rain slowly stopped.
And, the sun came out from behind the clouds.
A beautiful rainbow had formed.
And I looked at the ground because it was a sad reminder.

For, the one thing I saw that was so beautiful,
Had lost its glory.
I look back up and I think of her.
The one who fought so tirelessly.
And was brave til' the end.

When, she was well,
We were free as birds.
We would walk or ride our bikes,
And laugh all the while.
It seemed as though the fun wouldn't stop.

But, it rained,
It got dark and it rained hard.
We seeked shelter at her home
And waited til' once again we could play.
Then, the rain stopped.

We went outside,
and looked at the sky,
And we marveled at God's creation.
For when we looked,
We saw the colorful promise.

And as we looked, we pondered a thought,
Why does the rainbow run.
Theories upon theories, but,
no acceptable answer.

At that moment,
My auntie looked at me and smiled.
She said to me, one day,
We will figure out the secret.
We will chase rainbows.

Another day, came the rain.
But, the cancer came also.
And when the rain went away,
Her sickness stayed.

From beside her bed,
I held her hand.
She looked at me and said,
Darling don't be upset,
We can go chasing another day.

Now shes gone,
and I hang my head.
I look down and try to hide my face,
When I see the promise in the sky,
I think of the promise unfulfilled.
