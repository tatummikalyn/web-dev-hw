How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 TBS Milk
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional: Dried Parsley

Directions

  1. Place a small frying pan on the stove and preheat to Medium Low.
  2. Crack both eggs into a small bowl.
  3. Add milk
  4. Add a pinch of salt and pepper
  5. Whisk ingreadients together until combined resembling a light yellow color
  6. Add the egg mixture to the frying pan, scraping the bottom of the pan for 4 minutes as the eggs cook
  7. Plate your scrambeled eggs
  8. Garnish with dried parsely (optional)
  9. Enjoy
